Why Support Us ?

One major issue that we still face is a lack of reach. Your donation will help us expand our influence and ability to reach out to hundreds in need that are currently unaware of our existence. It’ll also help us get in touch with and recruit more certified counselors to our cause. Even the smallest donation makes a world of difference. Each donor is also sent a mental health awareness ribbon to help spread the word!
